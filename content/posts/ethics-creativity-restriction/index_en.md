I see these, and then on the other hand, I remember [Soheil Moghaddam's interview](https://t.me/dr_biglari_ds/2963) with Tabaqeh 16, only to get more infuriated. He was saying I have no problem with having an unethical person on my team. A person who doesn't consider his teammates as human beings and behaves like a jerk is acceptable to Mr. Moghaddam.

It gives me a headache seeing someone put ethics and decency in opposition to knowledge. Why should these two be placed against each other? I get a headache when someone thinks that creating an environment for unethical behavior and setting fire to the lives of the majority is worth nurturing elites. While following this massacre, which they call competition, so many people have become so frustrated that it has led to the inefficiency of a large number of talents who never had the chance to emerge. Or the rotten mindset that thinks people must take a beating to blossom!!! People must be given hardship to become creative!!!

It is very interesting that we have all of these to the highest degree in Iran, and after all these centuries, besides misery and backwardness, what exact blossoming have we had?! Anyone who could take a corner of the work has either left and is giving their energy somewhere else, or those who stayed can ultimately only keep their own surroundings clean. Then why can't we build DeepSeeks?! Why are we still riding donkeys?! By the gentlemen's definition, we should be at the highest economic-scientific levels! Don't you think that perhaps more important than these yellow, old-school nonsenses is the existence of an evolved system that makes America, America? Or makes Deng Xiaoping's China ultimately America's nightmare?
